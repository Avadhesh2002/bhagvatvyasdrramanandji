Spiritual Journey

Bhagvat Vyas Dr Ramanand Ji is a renowned spiritual leader dedicated to spreading the divine teachings of Shrimad Bhagwat Purana. With over 15 years of experience in delivering Bhagwat Kathas, he has touched the lives of thousands of devotees across India.

Born in a spiritually inclined family, Dr Ramanand Ji showed deep interest in scriptures from a young age. He received formal education in Sanskrit and Vedic literature, followed by rigorous training under esteemed gurus in the tradition of Bhagwat Katha exposition.
